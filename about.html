About us

Built for developers,
by developers.

Thezer0 Tools is a free, no-login collection of utilities for developers and internet users. Every tool runs entirely in your browser — no data ever leaves your device.

What is Thezer0 Tools?

We built Thezer0 Tools because we were tired of bloated websites full of ads, sign-up walls, and tools that upload your files to unknown servers. Every utility here does its job locally — in your browser tab — and nothing more.

Whether you need to format a JSON blob, generate a UUID, compress an image, or sort a list of lines, you can do it here in seconds without friction.
